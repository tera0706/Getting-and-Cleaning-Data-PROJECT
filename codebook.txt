Below are described all variables presented in data set. 

The features selected for this database come from the accelerometer and gyroscope 3-axial raw signals.
Prefix t mean that this is time domain signal.
Signals were captured at a constant rate of 50 Hz.
Signals were calculated using the Euclidean norm (tBodyAccMag, tGravityAccMag, tBodyAccJerkMag, tBodyGyroMag, tBodyGyroJerkMag)
